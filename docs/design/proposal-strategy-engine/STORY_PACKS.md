# Story Packs

Version: 40.0
Status: Design only. No production implementation.

## 1. Purpose

Story Pack defines the persuasive order of a proposal.

Presentation Pack decides visual expression. Story Pack decides narrative sequence.

Each Story Pack is designed as an eight-page proposal backbone that can be expanded into detailed decks.

## 2. Story Pack Summary

| Story Pack | Best for | Main promise |
|---|---|---|
| ROI Story | executives and budget approval | investment can be judged rationally |
| DX Story | cross-department transformation | operation and data are connected |
| AI Story | AI-centered projects | AI assists human decisions safely |
| Automation Story | RPA and workflow automation | repetitive work is moved to controlled automation |
| Quality Story | inspection, QA, OCR, recognition | quality variance is reduced and traceable |
| Customer Experience Story | Web, CRM, CX, support | customer experience and response speed improve |

## 3. ROI Story

| Page | Message |
|---|---|
| 1 Hero | This investment addresses a measurable business bottleneck. |
| 2 Executive Summary | Cost, time, and risk are framed as decision criteria. |
| 3 Current Cost Structure | Current burden is visible as labor, delay, rework, or opportunity loss. |
| 4 Improvement Lever | The proposal changes the process where value is lost. |
| 5 Implementation Plan | Phased rollout reduces investment risk. |
| 6 KPI Design | Baseline, target, and decision rule are defined before full rollout. |
| 7 Estimate and Risk | Cost range, assumptions, and risk controls are transparent. |
| 8 Decision Request | Approve PoC, budget range, and next decision date. |

## 4. DX Story

| Page | Message |
|---|---|
| 1 Hero | Digital operation connects people, data, and decisions. |
| 2 Executive Summary | Fragmented operations are reorganized into a controlled workflow. |
| 3 Current Fragmentation | Data, tools, and responsibility are scattered. |
| 4 Future Operating Model | Roles and data flow are clarified. |
| 5 Architecture | Systems, data, and human review are connected. |
| 6 Roadmap | Transformation proceeds by phase, not by big-bang change. |

## 5. AI Story

| Page | Message |
|---|---|

## 9. Story Selection Principles

- Select one primary story.
- Use one secondary story only when a compound project requires it.
- Do not mix more than two story patterns in an eight-page deck.
- The first three pages must match the primary audience.
- Technical pages must not appear before business value for executive audiences.
- Operational pages must not be hidden until the appendix for field audiences.
