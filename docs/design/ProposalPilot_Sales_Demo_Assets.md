## 8. Safety Checks

- No API keys are included.
- No Password, Token, Authorization, Cookie, or DATABASE_URL values are included.
- No production request IDs are included.
- No real customer names, real emails, real phone numbers, or confidential project text are included.
- No unsupported numeric claims such as `90%削減`, `10分で完成`, or `必ず受注率向上` are used as product claims.
- Beautiful.ai is described as an external service output option, not as a guaranteed result.
- AI output is described as support that requires human confirmation.

## 9. Export Notes

- PPTX slide size: 16:9 wide, 1280 x 720 px.
- PDF export: 12 pages, generated from rendered slide previews.
- Speaker notes are embedded in the PPTX.
- The PDF is intended for viewing and handoff, while the PPTX remains the editable master.
