## Quality checklist

- 8 slides, 16:9.
- One message per page.
- Different layout on every page.
- Editable PowerPoint-native text and shapes.
- No external references or embedded third-party assets.
- No unsupported result numbers.
- No web-production terms.
- No win probability.
- No lead-generation wording.

## Human approval criteria

Do not integrate this design into the production engine until human reviewers score the average at 4.0 or above across: first impression, premium feel, quick comprehension, diagram clarity, spacing, sales explanation, Copilot comparison, editability, customer submission quality, and ProposalPilot fit.
