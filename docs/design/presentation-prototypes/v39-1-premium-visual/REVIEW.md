## V39.1 Differentiators

- Every slide has a single visual lead.
- The AI Recognition Frame becomes a consistent signature motif.
- The deck alternates dark impact pages and light explanation pages.
- Human Review UI appears as a concrete operator experience.
- Learning loop is visible in the architecture and transformation story.
- V39.1 avoids treating premium design as only color, shadow, or card decoration.

## AI Recognition Expression

AI recognition appears on the cover, executive visual sequence, architecture, KPI dashboard, and scope gate. The recognition frame and floating tags connect the deck visually while changing purpose by page.

## Human and AI Role Expression

The deck repeatedly separates:

- AI: candidate detection, classification, routing, and learning.
- Human: confirmation, correction, approval, and final operational judgment.

This prevents the proposal from overclaiming full automation.

## Learning Loop

The learning loop appears as a feedback path from approved or corrected results into learning and operations. It is shown most clearly in the Before / After and Architecture pages.

## V39 Reuse Explanation

V39 content and story are intentionally reused to preserve information design continuity. Layouts, backgrounds, visual leads, recognition motifs, UI mock intensity, and page rhythm are changed for V39.1.

## Quality Notes

- The PPTX is designed with editable PowerPoint-native objects.
- No external visual assets are embedded.
- The PDF is generated from the rendered prototype pages for review convenience.
- PNG pages and comparison images are provided for human visual approval.

## Human Approval Gate

Human reviewers should score the following before production integration:

- First impression
- Premium feel
- Visual lead clarity in 3 seconds
- Content understanding in 10 seconds
- Diagram clarity
- Typography and readability
- Spacing
- Rhythm across pages
- Sales explanation quality
- Copilot comparison
- Editability
- Customer submission quality
- ProposalPilot fit
- Memorability

Do not integrate into the production PPTX engine until the average human score reaches 4.3 or above.
