# Version 37.2 Prototype Review Guide

## Purpose

Compare two premium visual directions before selecting any direction for the production PPTX engine. These files are prototypes only and are not wired into the product.

## Review Target

Scenario: 生花オークション向けAI画像認識案件

Slides in each prototype:

1. 表紙
2. 現状課題
3. Before / After 業務フロー
4. AI画像認識システム構成
5. PoC計画・スケジュール
6. 概算見積・次のアクション

## Design A: Editorial Premium

A uses a darker, editorial treatment with large typography, dramatic contrast, and fewer modular UI elements. It is suited for executive-facing proposal openings and high-value sales conversations where confidence and narrative impact matter.

## Design B: Modern SaaS Visual

B uses a cleaner SaaS-like card system, light surfaces, process cards, and dashboard-style information structure. It is suited for product demos, operational stakeholders, and repeatable proposal production.

## Review Criteria

- Does the first slide feel premium enough for customer-facing sales?
- Can the audience understand the business issue within 10 seconds?
- Is the Before / After flow easy to explain aloud?
- Does the architecture slide clarify rather than overwhelm?
- Does the PoC slide make the next decision concrete?
- Does the estimate/action slide avoid overclaiming and preserve editability?

## Decision Rule

Do not merge either direction into the production presentation engine until a human reviewer approves the preferred direction and any required edits.
