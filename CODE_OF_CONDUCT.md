# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We aim to maintain a professional, respectful, and safe collaboration environment for everyone working on AI営業秘書.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Protect customer, company, and personal information.
- Avoid sharing secrets, credentials, tokens, or confidential data.
- Report security concerns responsibly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing API keys, passwords, tokens, or customer confidential text
- Intentionally bypassing access controls
- Disruptive or unsafe operational behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior puts people, customers, or production systems at risk.
